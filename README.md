Key Business Insights 
1. United Kingdom Generates the Highest Revenue 
● The United Kingdom contributes by far the largest share of total sales. 
● Revenue from the UK is approximately 8.29 million, significantly higher than every other 
region. 
● This indicates that the UK is the company's strongest and most established market. 
2. International Markets Have Growth Potential 
● After the UK, the highest revenue-generating regions are: 
○ Netherlands 
○ EIRE (Ireland) 
○ Germany 
○ France 
● Although these markets generate lower sales than the UK, they represent strong 
opportunities for business expansion through targeted marketing and product availability.

4. Focus on High-Performing Product Categories 
● Products in Home Decor, Kitchen & Dining, and Bags consistently generate strong 
sales. 
● Maintaining inventory and launching promotional campaigns for these categories can 
further increase revenue. 
5. Opportunity to Improve Sales in Smaller Markets 
● Regions outside the UK contribute a much smaller share of overall revenue. 
● Expanding localized promotions, improving shipping options, or introducing 
region-specific product recommendations could help increase sales in these markets. 
Actionable Recommendations 
● Continue investing in the UK market, as it remains the primary revenue source. 
● Expand marketing efforts in Germany, France, Netherlands, and Ireland to increase 
international sales. 
● Prioritize inventory for Other / General Gifts, Home Decor, and Kitchen & Dining 
products. 
● Create seasonal bundles and promotional campaigns around the highest-selling product 
categories. 
● Analyze lower-performing regions to identify barriers such as pricing, logistics, or limited 
product availability.
